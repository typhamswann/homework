Inequality Practice Problems

Objective: This handout is designed to help students practice solving one-step inequalities.

One-Step Inequalities:

  1. x + 4 > 9
  2. y - 6 < 7
  3. 5z ≥ 25
  4. m/3 ≤ 6

Challenge: Multiplying and Dividing by a Negative Number

  1. -2x ≤ 10
  2. y/-4 > 2

Word Problems:

  1. Sarah has 15 candies in her bag. She wants to buy more candies so that she has at least 25 candies in total. How many candies does Sarah need to buy?

  2. John has saved $80 so far. He wants to buy a video game that costs $150. How much more money does he need to save in order to buy the video game?

Remember to check your work and ask your teacher for help if needed.
